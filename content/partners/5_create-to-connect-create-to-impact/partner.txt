Partnerdescription:

Create to Connect -> Create to Impact (CtC -> CtI) is funded through the Creative Europe programme of the European Union. It is a joint effort of sixteen European cultural and research organizations to create impact in the society in terms of change, innovation, empowerment and emancipation through building powerful and long-lasting connections of artists, cultural operators, researchers and audience.
